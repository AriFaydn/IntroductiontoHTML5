Individual and Team Sports

Sports play an important role in physical health, mental well‑being, and social development. Some sports are practiced individually, while others require teamwork and cooperation.

Individual Sports

These sports focus on personal performance and self‑discipline.

  • Running
  • Swimming
  • Tennis

Team Sports

These sports require cooperation, communication, and shared strategy.

  • Football
  • Basketball
  • Volleyball
